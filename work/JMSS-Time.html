JMSS Time

Modernisation and feature development for a prexisting online timetable.

JMSS Time was a website originally created in 2017, with the simple aim of letting John Monash Science School students know when their classes are. Collective Fullstack were brought in to update the website for the timetable introduced in 2020, as well to add new features.
